    • Discourse on Progressive Enhancement

    The term, progressive enhancement, refers to a paradigm shift in organizational systems from an engineering viewpoint to a content perspective in the web development industry. Now considered a traditional method of web development, progressive enhancement focuses on the content being delivered by a website, rather than being concerned about engineering capabilities such as primary browser limitations. This progressive enhancement paradigm shift is evident across multiple industries and products when one views various product development cycles in entrepreneurial organizations. In general, the engineers first develop a product with which to enter the marketplace. Once the product is established, the marketers seek to increase the technology base to be as applicable as possible in seeking interested viewers for revenue exploitation.
